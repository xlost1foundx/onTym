Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our commercial maintenance services.

Do you provide services during business hours?

We specialize in after-hours service to minimize disruption to your business operations. However, we can accommodate daytime scheduling when requested and coordinate with your business needs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and carry comprehensive general liability insurance. We can provide certificates of insurance upon request for your peace of mind and property protection.

How often should I schedule lot sweeping?

The frequency depends on your property's traffic and location. High-traffic areas typically benefit from weekly service, while moderate-traffic properties may only need bi-weekly or monthly maintenance. We'll assess your specific needs during our consultation.

What areas do you service?

We serve commercial properties throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Do you offer emergency or one-time services?

Absolutely! While we specialize in recurring maintenance contracts, we're happy to provide one-time services or emergency cleanup as needed. Emergency services may include additional fees based on timing and scope.

How do I get a quote?

Getting a quote is easy! Use our online quote form, call us directly, or use our quick estimate calculator above. We'll schedule a free consultation to assess your property and provide a detailed, customized quote.
